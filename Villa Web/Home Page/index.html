Welcome

to Villa Della Torre

“Villa Della Torre: a jewel of the Italian Renaissance

immersed in the timeless beauty of Valpolicella”

Owned by the Mastella Allegrini family since 2008, Villa Della Torre was created by the Masters Giulio Romano and Michele Sanmicheli, who designed its various parts, according to the model of the ancient Roman ‘domus’. Today it houses an Art&Wine Relais and is home to the Villa Della Torre winery.

A true Italian-style Château-Relais, Villa Della Torre is a magical place where the rituals of fine wine and local culture of food are celebrated with passion, simplicity and elegance as part of an immersive experience that combines respect for art, culture and natural beauty. Villa Della Torre is an ideal venue for weddings and religious celebrations, corporate meetings, film shoots, exhibitions and cultural events, music concerts, fashion shows and photo shoots. We also provide all our indoor and outdoor spaces, on an exclusive basis, for our events.
